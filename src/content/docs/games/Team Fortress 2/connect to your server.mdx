Here is how you can join your [Team Fortress 2 server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/team-fortress-2-server-hosting/) by using the IP address.

## Connecting Using the Console
<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your TF2 server.
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open Team Fortress 2.
4. Open the `Developer Console (~)`, type connect then paste the copied server IP, and press Enter to connect.
</Steps>

## Connecting With The Steam Browser 

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open Steam and at the top click `View > Game Servers`.
4. Click `Favorites` and select `Team Fortress 2` as the game.
5. Click the Plus (+) Icon and add your `[ServerIP:Port]`.
6. Connect to your server.
</Steps>
