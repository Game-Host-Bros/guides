At Game Host Bros changing Max Players on your new [Team Fortress 2 server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/team-fortress-2-server-hosting/) is quick and easy. here's how to change Max Players on your TF2 Server.
<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
