Changing Max Players on your new Factorio server is quick and easy. At Game Host Bros here's how to change Max Players on your new Factorio Server.

## Setting Max Players For Factorio

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
