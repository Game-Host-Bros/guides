Changing Max Players on your new [Arma Reforger server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/arma-reforger-server-hosting/) is quick and easy. Here's how to change Max Players on your Arma Reforger Server.

## Setting Max Players

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Basic`, edit `Max Players`.
4. Restart your Server.
</Steps>
