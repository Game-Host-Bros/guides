In this guide you will learn how to enable Crossplay on your [Arma Reforger server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/arma-reforger-server-hosting/). This will allow you to play with players on Steam, Xbox and Playstation.

## Enabling Crossplay

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Advanced`, set Crossplay to `True`.
4. Restart your server.
</Steps>
