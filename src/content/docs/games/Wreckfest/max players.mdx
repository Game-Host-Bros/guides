If you're trying to change the amount of friends allowed in the chaos, at Game Host Bros we've made it a quick and easy process. Here's how to change Max Players on your new Wreckfest Server.

1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
