Many of the things you may want to do on your [Minecraft Bedrock server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/minecraft-server-hosting/) will require Administrator/OP permissions, luckily it's very easy to become an Admin, managing players and entering creative mode has never been easier!

Here is how you can make yourself an admin on your Minecraft Bedrock server.

## Adding A Server OP

<Steps>
  1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Minecraft server.
  2. Go to `System > Console`.
  3. Type `op Username`.
  4. Connect to your server.
</Steps>

If you're an OP with [cheats enabled](/games/minecraft-bedrock/enable-cheats/), you can use these commands while connected to your server.

## Removing A Server OP

<Steps>
  1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Minecraft server.
  2. Go to `System > Console`.
  3. Type `deop Username`.
</Steps>
