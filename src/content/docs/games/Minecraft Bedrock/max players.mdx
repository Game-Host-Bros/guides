Need more players? Or looking to limit users without creating a whitelist? Here's how you can set Max Players on your [Minecraft Bedrock server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/minecraft-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For Minecraft Bedrock

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Minecraft server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players` to your desired value.  
4. Start your Server.
</Steps>
