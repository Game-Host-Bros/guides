At Game Host Bros we make it easy to adjust Max Players. Here's how you can easily adjust player slots on your new American Truck Simulator Server to get all of your friends together on the road!

1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
