In this guide we will show you how you can add moderators to your [American Truck Simulator server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/american-truck-simulator-server-hosting/). For this you will need the [steamID64](https://steamid.io/) of the players you want to make mods.

## Adding Moderators In American Truck Simulator

<Steps>
1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the `Moderators` and add the steamID64 of the player you want to be a mod.
4. Restart your server.
</Steps>
You can also add multiple moderator steamID64 by separating them with a comma like this:
```text
76561198012345678,745611980123454432
```
