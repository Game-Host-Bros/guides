Here's how to connect to your [The Lord of the Rings Return to Moria server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/the-lord-of-the-rings-return-to-moria-server-hosting/).

## Connecting To Your LotR Return To Moria Server

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your server.
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. In LotR Return To Moria select your character.
4. Click `Join Other World > Advanced Join Options`.
5. Under `Direct Join`, paste your Server IP address and click `Join Server`.
</Steps>

If you get stuck at any time, you can reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/FN4XgbAwzr).
