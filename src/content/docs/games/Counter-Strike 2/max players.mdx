We make adjusting player slots quick and easy, if you're looking to adjust player slots on your [CS2 server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/cs2-server-hosting/) here's how it's done.

## Setting Max Players

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
