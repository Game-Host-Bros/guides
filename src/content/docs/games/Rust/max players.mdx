At Game Host Bros we make changing Max Players quick and easy. Here's how to change Max Players on your [Rust server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/rust-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For Rust

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
