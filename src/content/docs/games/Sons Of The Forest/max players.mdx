By default, we have the Max Players for SOTF as `8` which is the games maximum. If you're trying to play with a smaller group of people but don't feel like adding a password, here's how to change max players on your [Sons Of The Forest server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/sons-of-the-forest-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For Sons Of The Forest

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
