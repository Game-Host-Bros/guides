Valheim lets you change the difficulty of certian things in game by setting World Modifiers. You can adjust any of the available world modifers directly from our Game Panel.

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the World Modifer setting you want to adjust from the drop down menus.
- Combat
- Resources
- Death Penalty
- Raid Rate
- Portals
4. Restart your server.
