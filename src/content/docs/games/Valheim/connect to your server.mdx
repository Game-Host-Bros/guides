Here's how to connect to your [Valheim Server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/valheim-server-hosting/), there are two methods to do so, one being in-game and the other being through the Steam browser, here's how to do both.

## Connecting To Your Server
<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. In Valheim click `Start Game` and `Select` your character.
4. Press `Join Game` and `Add Server`.
5. Enter your IP address and click `Connect`.
</Steps>
## Connecting Using The Steam Browser

:::tip
Did you know? You can use the Steam browser to open Valheim and automatically connect.
:::
<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open Steam and at the top click `View > Game Servers`.
4. Click `Favorites` and select Valheim as the game.
5. Click the Plus (+) Icon and add your `[ServerIP:Port+1]`, for example change `[YourIP]:27016` to `[YourIP]:27017`.
6. Connect to your server.
</Steps>
