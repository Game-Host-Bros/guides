By default, Max Players will be set to 32 on your [Palworld server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/palworld-server-hosting/). If you're looking to increase, or even lower the amount of players on your server, here's how to do it.

## Setting Max Players For Palworld

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Basic` edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
