Setting up a password on your [Palworld server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/palworld-server-hosting/) is essential for maintaining control over who can access your game world. Whether you're hosting a private session for friends or ensuring only authorized players join your server, this quick guide will walk you through the simple steps needed to add a password. 

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Palworld server.
2. Click `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Look for `Server Password` and then type the password you want.
4. Go back to the console page, then restart your server.
</Steps>
