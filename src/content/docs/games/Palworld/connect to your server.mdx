Here is how you can join your Palworld Dedicated Server using the IP address.

## How to Connect to Your PC Palworld Server 

1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Click on your Palworld Server.
3. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
4. Open Palworld and click `Join Multiplayer Game`
5. At the bottom of the screen you will see `127.0.0.1:8211`, replace this with your IP address and click `Connect`

## How to Connect to Your Xbox Palworld Server

1. Name your Server in the Panel by following [This Guide](https://guides.gamehostbros.com/games/palworld/change-server-name/)
2. Select `Join Multiplayer Game` in the Palworld menu.
3. Select `Community Servers`
4. Search your Servers name in the box located at the top.
5. Once found, select your Server to connect.

:::tip
It may take a couple of minutes for your Server to show up.
:::
