Starting fresh in [Palworld](https://www.gamehostbros.com/palworld-server-hosting/) can be an exciting opportunity to explore new adventures, build different structures, and experience the game from a whole new perspective. Whether you're dealing with world corruption, or simply looking for a clean slate, resetting your world is quick and straightforward.

## Resetting Worlds

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and stop your Palworld server.
2. In the sidebar go to `Management > File Manager`.
3. Once there navigate to `Pal/Saved/SaveGames/0/`.
4. Delete the folder with random numbers like `84B5E2264EC387DEDB43179D666031A1`.
5. Start your server.
</Steps>
