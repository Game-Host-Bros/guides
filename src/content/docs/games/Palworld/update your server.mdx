This is how you can update your server after a Palworld update. 

1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Click on your Palworld server.
3. From the console, click the Restart button. 

## My Palworld Server Isn't Updating

If for some reason you can't connect to your Palworld server after an update, your Auto Updates may be disabled. This is how you can re-enable the updates for your server. 

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Select your server.
3. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
4. Find the `Auto Update` setting and change the value to `1`.
5. Restart your server.
