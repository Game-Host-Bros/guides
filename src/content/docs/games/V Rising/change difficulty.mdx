In this guide, we will show you how to change the server difficulty on your V Rising server.

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Select your V Rising server.
3. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
4. Find the `Game Difficulty Preset` setting and change it to the desired difficulty.
5. Restart your server.
