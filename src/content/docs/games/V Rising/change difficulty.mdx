In this guide, we will show you how to change the server difficulty on your V Rising server.

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your V Rising server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the `Game Difficulty Preset` setting and change it to the desired difficulty.
4. Restart your server.
