If you're trying to add more Vampires to the fun, or even cut down on the existing Vampire population. Here's how you can change Max Players on your [V Rising server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/v-rising-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For V Rising

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Connected Users`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
