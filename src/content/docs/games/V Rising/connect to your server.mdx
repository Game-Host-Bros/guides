Here is how you can join your V Rising dedicated server using the IP address.

## How to Connect Using the Console
<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your V Rising server.
2. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open V Rising.
4. Click `Play > Online Play > Show All Servers > Direct Connect`.
5. Paste your game server IP address and then click connect.
</Steps>
