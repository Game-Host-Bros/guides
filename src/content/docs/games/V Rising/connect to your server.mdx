Here is how you can join your V Rising dedicated server using the IP address.

## How to Connect Using the Console

1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Click on your V Rising server.
3. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
4. Open V Rising.
5. Click `Play > Online Play > Show All Servers > Direct Connect`.
6. Paste your game server IP address and then click connect.
