If you don't want to play V Rising on the latest public version, and would rather play an older release of the game, here's how to change the version of your [V Rising server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/v-rising-server-hosting/). 

:::note
This is required to play worlds from 1.0 and prior versions on a V Rising server, furthermore you **must** [direct connect by IP](/games/v-rising/connect-to-your-server/) to join outdated servers.
:::

## Changing Steam Beta Version

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Steam Beta Branch`.
4. Go to `Configuration > Advanced`.
5. Click `Reinstall Server` and wait for the install to finish.
</Steps>

Here are the currently available beta branches to choose from, they can be found here:

- [V Rising Beta Branches](https://steamdb.info/app/1829350/depots/#branches).
