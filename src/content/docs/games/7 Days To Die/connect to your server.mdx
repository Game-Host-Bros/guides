Here is how you can join your [7D2D dedicated server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/7-days-to-die-server-hosting/) using the IP address.

## Connecting Through The In-Game Server Browser :badge[Recommended]{variant=success}

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your 7D2D server.
2. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open 7D2D.
4. Click `Join A Game` then `Connect To IP`.
5. Enter the server IP address and port from your server and click Connect.
</Steps>

## Connecting With The Steam Browser 

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open Steam and at the top click `View > Game Servers`.
4. Click `Favorites` and select `7 Days To Die` as the game.
5. Click the Plus (+) Icon and add your `[ServerIP:Port]`.
6. Connect to your server.
</Steps>
