In this guide we will show you to adjust the amount of players that can join your 7 Days To Die Server. Please note that anything set in our Game Panel overwrites anything set in your `serverconfig.xml` file.

## Setting Max Players

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
