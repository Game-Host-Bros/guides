In this guide we will show you to adjust the amount of players that can join your [7 Days To Die Server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/7-days-to-die-server-hosting/). Please note that anything set in our Game Panel overwrites anything set in your `serverconfig.xml` file.

## Setting Max Players For 7D2D

<Steps>

1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Basic` Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
