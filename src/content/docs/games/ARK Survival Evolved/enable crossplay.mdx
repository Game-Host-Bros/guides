This is what you do if you need to enable crossplay on your [Ark Survival Evolved server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/ark-survival-evolved-server-hosting/), this allows players from Steam and Epic Games to play together.

## Enabling Crossplay 

<Steps>
1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Ark server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the `Additional Arguments` setting and enter `-crossplay`.
4. Restart your server.
</Steps>
