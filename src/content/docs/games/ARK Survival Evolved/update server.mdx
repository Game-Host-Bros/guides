Here are the steps to update your [ARK Survival Evolved server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/ark-survival-evolved-server-hosting/).

## Updating Your ARK Survival Evolved Server

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the `Automatic Update` setting and make sure it's enabled.
4. `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
