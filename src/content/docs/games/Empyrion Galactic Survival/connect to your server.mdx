Here's how to connect to your [Empyrion Galactic Survival server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/empyrion-galactic-survival-server-hosting/).

## Direct Connecting To Your Server 

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Empyrion server.
2. Copy the [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. In Empyrion select `Multiplayer`.
4. Enter your Server IP address into `Direct IP and Port`.
5. Click `Connect`.
</Steps>

:::note
If you enable EAC (Easy Anti Cheat), then all players **must** also enable it to join your server.
:::

If you get stuck at any time, you can reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/FN4XgbAwzr).
