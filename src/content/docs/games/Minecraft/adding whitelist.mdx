This is what you do if you need to add a whitelist to your Minecraft Server.

## Adding Players To The Whitelist

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Select your server.
3. Go to `System > Console`.
4. Type `whitelist add [Username]`.

## Enabling And Reloading The Whitelist

While still in `System > Console`. Enter these commands to Enable and Reload your servers whitelist.

1. Type `whitelist on`.
2. Type `whitelist reload`.
3. Restart your server.

You can type `whitelist list` to view your whitelist, and `whitelist off` to disable it.

:::tip
To remove players from your Whitelist, enter the command `whitelist remove [Username]` and then `whitelist reload`.
:::
