This is what you do if you want to change the difficulty of your [Minecraft server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/minecraft-server-hosting/), changing your Minecraft server difficulty is the fastest way to adjust your survival experience, whether you'd like to make it more brutal, or instead enjoy a peaceful and scenic playthrough without any enemies in sight, you can do so by following this guide.

## Changing Your Minecraft Server Difficulty

<Steps>
  1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and stop your Minecraft server.
  2. Go to `Configuration > File Manager`.
  3. Open `server.properties`, edit `difficulty=` to one of the values below and then click save.  
     - `peaceful`, `easy`, `normal`, `hard`
  4. Start your Server.
</Steps>
