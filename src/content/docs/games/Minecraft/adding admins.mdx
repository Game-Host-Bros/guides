Many of the things you may want to do on your Minecraft server will require Administrator/OP permissions, luckily it's very easy to become an Admin, managing players and entering creative mode has never been easier!

Here is how you can make yourself an admin on your Minecraft server.

<Steps>
  1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
  2. Select your server.
  3. Go to `System > Console`.
  4. Type `op [YourUsername]`.
  5. Connect to your server.
</Steps>
