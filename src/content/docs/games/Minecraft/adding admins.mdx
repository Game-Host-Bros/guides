Here is how you can make yourself an admin on your Minecraft server.

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Select your server.
3. Go to `System > Console`.
4. Type `op [YourUsername]`.
5. Connect to your server.
