Need more players? Or looking to limit users without creating a whitelist? Here's how you can set Max Players on your [Minecraft server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/minecraft-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For Minecraft

<Steps>
  1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Minecraft server.
  2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
  3. Look for `Max Players` and set it to the amount you want.  
  4. Start your Server.
</Steps>
