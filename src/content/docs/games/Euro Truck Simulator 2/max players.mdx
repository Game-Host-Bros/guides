At Game Host Bros we make it easy to adjust Max Players. Here's how you can easily adjust player slots on your new [Euro Truck Simulator 2 server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/euro-truck-simulator-2-server-hosting/) to get all of your friends together on the road!

## Setting Max Players For ETS 2

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>

:::danger
For player counts above 8 you MUST modify your local game config files otherwise you won't be able to see your server in the browser, how to do that can be found in the section below.
:::

## More Than 8 Players - 128 Players ETS 2 Convoy

If you want to have more than 8 players on your ETS 2 server every player MUST modify their local game files to allow for higher player count servers, otherwise they won't be able to see or join your server, here's how that's done.

These steps are to be followed after changing Max Players in `Configuration > Startup Parameters` and restarting your server in the steps above.

**Windows PC**
<Steps>
1. Close your ETS 2 Game
2. Press `Windows Key + R` and paste this command.
   ```
   %USERPROFILE%\Documents\Euro Truck Simulator 2
   ```
3. Edit `config.cfg` with notepad.
4. Press `Crtl + F` to find `uset g_max_convoy_size` and change it's value to 128, it should now look like this.
   - uset g_max_convoy_size "128"
5. Save your changes and start ETS 2.
</Steps>
