At Game Host Bros we make it easy to adjust Max Players. Here's how you can easily adjust player slots on your new Euro Truck Simulator 2 Server to get all of your friends together on the road!
<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
