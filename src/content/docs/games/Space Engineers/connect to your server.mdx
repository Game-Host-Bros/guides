Here is how you can connect to your [Space Engineers server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/space-engineers-server-hosting/) from the in-game server browser.

## Connecting To Your Server

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your Space Engineers server.
2. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
3. Open Space Engineers and click the `Join Game` button.
   - If you get a popup for Quick Start, select No.
4. Select `Direct Connect`.
5. Paste in your IP address and click `Connect`.
</Steps>
