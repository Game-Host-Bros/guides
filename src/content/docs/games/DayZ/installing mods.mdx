Adding mods to DayZ is a great way to mix-up the gameplay and try new things, whether it's a simple quality-of-life mod or a total overhaul, there'll likely be something that's perfect for you. This guide will cover where to find and add mods to your [DayZ server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/dayz-server-hosting/).

## Finding DayZ Mods

You can [view all the DayZ mods here](https://steamcommunity.com/app/221100/workshop/).

Browse the Steam Workshop and `Subscribe` to the mods you want to add.

## Exporting Your DayZ Modlist 

<Steps>
1. Open DayZ
2. In the launcher click `Mods`.
3. Select `More > Export list of mods to a file`.
4. Choose `Only Loaded` or `All Mods`.
</Steps>


## Installing Mods To Your DayZ Server

Now that you've exported your modlist, here's how to install these mods on your server.

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Game Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and select your DayZ Server.
2. Go to `Management > File Manager`.
3. [Upload](/getting-started/uploading-files/) your exported `modlist.html`.
4. Start your server.
</Steps>

The mods will automatically update, download and enable once your server starts, users can download these mods while connecting to your server.
