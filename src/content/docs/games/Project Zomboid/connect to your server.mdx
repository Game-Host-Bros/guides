Here is how you can join your [Project Zomboid server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/project-zomboid-server-hosting/).

## Favoriting Your Server In-Game :badge[Recommended]{variant=success}

This is the recommended method to use when connecting to your Project Zomboid server.

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and copy your [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
2. Open Project Zomboid, in the main menu select `Join > Favorites`.
3. Paste your Server IP address into the IP field, then move the Port into the `Port` field, IP and Port should now look like this.
   - IP: YourIP (ex. 111.111.111.111)             
   - Port: YourPort (ex. 27015)
4. Enter a Username and Password, then click `Save`
5. Select your server and click `Join Server`.
</Steps>

## Using Steam Browser Favorites

This is how to connect through the Steam browser.

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and copy your [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
2. Open Steam and at the top click `View > Game Servers`.
3. Click `Favorites` and select `Project Zomboid` as the game.
4. Click the Plus (+) Icon and paste your [Server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg).
   - After selecting your server, click connect and enter your server password if applicable.
5. Once in-game enter a Username and Password, then join your server.
</Steps>

If you're having trouble joining your server once in-game, try enabling Use Steam Relay.
