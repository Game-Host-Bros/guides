This guide will walk you through the process of installing Steam Workshop mods on your [L4D2 server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/left-4-dead-2-server-hosting/).

## Installing Workshop Mods

<Steps>
1. Subscribe to the [Steam Workshop](https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/browse/?appid=550) items you want to install.
2. Right click on Left 4 Dead 2 in steam and select `Properties > Browse Local Files`.
3. Navigate to `left4dead2\addons` locally on your pc and you should see a list of your installed mods.
4. Connect to your server using [SFTP](/getting-started/uploading-files/) and navigate to `left4dead2/addons`.
5. Copy the folders from your PC to your server. 
6. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and restart your L4D2 server.
</Steps>
