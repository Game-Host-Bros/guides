By default, Game Host Bros [Necesse servers](https://www.gamehostbros.com/necesse-server-hosting/) will have updates disabled as updates may break your saves, if you want to update here are the steps to update your Necesse Server.

## Updating Your Necesse Server

<Steps>
1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Find the `Automatic Update` setting and make sure it's enabled.
4. `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
