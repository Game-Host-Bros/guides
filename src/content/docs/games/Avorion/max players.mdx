Changing Max Players on your new [Avorion server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/avorion-server-hosting/) is quick and easy. Here's how to change Max Players on your Avorion Server.

## Setting Max Players

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Avorion Server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Basic`, find the `Max Players` setting and enter your desired server name.
4. Start your server.
</Steps>
