Here is how you can join your Enshrouded dedicated server using the IP address.

1. Access the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Click on your Enshrouded server.
3. Copy the [server IP address](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ap7DtDF-wYg) from the sidebar.
4. Open Steam and click `View > Game Servers`.
5. In the server browser, click the Plus (+) icon and enter your IP address.
6. Click `Connect`
