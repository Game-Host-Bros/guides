Like many general server settings, at Game Host Bros setting Max Players will be done directly through our game panel, here's the quick and easy process to change Max Players on your new Ark Survival Evolved Server.
<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
