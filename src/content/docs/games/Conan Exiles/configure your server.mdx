In this guide, we will show you how you can edit the settings on your [Conan Exiles server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/conan-exiles-server-hosting/). 

## Configuring Your Conan Exiles Server

<Steps>
1. Firstly, login to the [Game Host Bros Game Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and stop your server.
2. Once stopped, on the left, navigate to `Management` then `File Manager`.
3. Navigate to `/ConanSandbox/Saved/Config/WindowsServer/ServerSettings.ini`.
4. After you have made the changes you want, start the server. 
</Steps>
