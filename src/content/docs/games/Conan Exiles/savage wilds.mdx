This is how to change the map on your [Conan Exiles server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/conan-exiles-server-hosting/) to [Savage Wilds](https://savagewilds.com/), a popular custom map found on the Steam Workshop.

## Installing Savage Wilds

<Steps>

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and stop your server.
2. Go to `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Add the ID `2377569193` to Workshop Mods.
</Steps>

## Loading Savage Wilds

Here's how to load the Savage Wilds map on your Conan Exiles server.

<Steps>

1. Log in to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and stop your server.
2. Go to `Management > File Manager`.
3. Navigate to `ConanSandbox/Saved/Config/WindowsServer/Engine.ini`
4. Add the following, separate from other sections.
    ```
   [/Script/EngineSettings.GameMapsSettings]
   ServerDefaultMap=/Game/Mods/Savage_Wilds/Savage_Wilds
   ```
5. Click save, then start your server.
</Steps>

That's it, now your server will be hosting the Savage Wilds map.
