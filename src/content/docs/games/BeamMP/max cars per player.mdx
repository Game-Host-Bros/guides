Not to be confused with AI cars, or Max Players. Max Cars Per Player will determine the amount of cars that can be spawned by a given player, this is how to adjust Max Cars Per Player on a [BeamMP Server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/beammp-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Cars Per Player For BeamMP

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Under `Basic`, edit `Max Cars Per Player`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>

:::note
If you have CobaltEssentials installed it MUST be configured otherwise it will override this setting with a default value of 1 for normal players.
:::
