Not to be confused with AI cars. Max Players will determine the amount of player cars that can join your server, here's how to adjust player slots on your [BeamMP Server](https://www.gamehostbros.com/beammp-server-hosting/).

## Setting Max Players For BeamMP

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Game Host Bros Panel](https://panel.gamehostbros.com/) and `Stop` your Server.
2. Select `Configuration > Startup Parameters`.
3. Edit `Max Players`.
4. After changing, `Start` your Server.
</Steps>
