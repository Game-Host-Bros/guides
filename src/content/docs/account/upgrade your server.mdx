At Game Host Bros, we understand the importance of flexibility. That's why we allow you to upgrade your server when extra resources are required. Upgrades are automatic and instant, so you can get right back to playing as soon as possible!s

<Steps>
1. Login to the [Billing Portal](https://portal.gamehostbros.com/).
2. Under Your `Active Products/Services`, look for the service you want to upgrade and click `VIEW DETAILS`.
3. In the sidebar on the left, select `Actions > Upgrade/Downgrade Options`.
4. Select the location you want and then press `Continue`.
</Steps>
